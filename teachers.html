A Teacher's Guide to "Good Fire vs. Bad Fire"

A visual walkthrough of the interactive lesson plan, from warm-up to certification.

Core Learning Objectives

Grades K-2

Students will be able to identify that some fires can be **helpful** ("Good Fire") for plants and animals, and will learn **basic fire safety rules**.

Grades 3-5

Students will be able to **explain the difference** between a prescribed burn and a wildfire, identify the **fire triangle**, and understand basic **weather conditions** for a safe burn.
